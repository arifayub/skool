Our School

Affiliated to CISCE (www.abc.org) G N Public School, Gorakhpur is a reputed English Medium School,established and governed by the Gorakhnath mandir. It is affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations, New Delhi. It was founded on 10th April 1997 with 90 students in Class VI and five members in the teaching staff. It runs classes from Lower Kinder Garten (LKG) to Class XII. the school has the primary objective of educating children of the community. However, we shall not spare any effort in rendering our services to all people, irrespective of religion, caste and creed and gender, so as to conserve their identity, language and culture. Our aim is the pursuit of excellence for all.
